Kerala

Munnar, Idukki

A Maheshwari Retreats Managed Resort
The murmur of Munnar can be heard far and wide - the rustle of tea leaves, the soft ripple of the lake, the gush of waterfalls and the wind whispering over the lush landscapes. It is in this setting - where serenity and spice mingle - that you will find Maheshwari Retreats Munnar Resort in Kerala. Our grand, cream toned resort, with its vibrant green roofs and grand entrance, stands out against a backdrop of thick green trees and blue skies. Surrounding our family resort in Munnar are tranquil tea estates and the calm Anayirangal Dam Lake. But wait! Before you close your eyes and drift off into dreams of mountains and mist, let us tell you a bit more about your time with us in Munnar. We will immerse you in bold flavours, brave adventures, rejuvenating ayurvedic treatments, rollicking DJ nights, jungle picnics and tree house dining.

Luxurious Amenities Offered

Kerala cuisine doesn’t hold back, and neither should you. Full-bodied flavours, mouthwatering ingredients and sassy spices. Join us at Barbeque Bay, where you can enjoy the tender, spiced Kozhikodan Chicken Roast, a thick Alleppey Fish Curry ladled over rice, and chunks of bold Mutton Chukka. If you are looking for fresh seafood, then you’re in the right place - plump prawns, squid and fresh fish drizzled, marinated and grilled to perfection. We also serve dishes from South Indian, North Indian, Continental and Chinese cuisines. Take a break from the holiday madness. Slow down at our Ayurvedic Spa and enjoy treatments like Snehana Abhyanga, herbal Kizhi therapy, our signature scrub, Svaastha Nirvana therapy and more. These treatments are enriched with herbs, oils and other natural ingredients that rejuvenate and relax you.
